Cool Resturant
Cool Restaurant has always been the definition of delicious and authentic K'un L'un cuisine. The founder Frank Castle survived a devistating plane crash. Barely alive, he was rescued by villagers from a nearby village. He then spent the next 15 years training in fine Tibetan cuisine. After his training he dispatched all the villagers and made his way back to America. He settled in Detroit which is known as food connoisseurs paradise. Thats when he opened the great establishment we know today as Cool Restaurant.
